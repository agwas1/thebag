Our Story

About TheBag

TheBag is Kenya's premier YouTube DJ series, born from the vibrant streets of Nairobi. We started with a simple mission: to showcase the incredible talent of Kenyan DJs and bring their mixes to the world.

From gengetone to amapiano, afrobeats to benga, our DJs curate the freshest mixes that capture the energy, culture, and sound of modern Kenya. Every playlist is a journey through the heartbeat of East Africa's music scene.
